Frequently Asked Questions
What is IPO?

IPO (Initial Public Offering) is the process through which a private company offers shares to the public for the first time. It provides an opportunity for the company to raise capital from public investors.

How do I calculate IPO allotment chances?

The IPO probability is calculated based on factors like investment amount, investor category, and the number of PANs involved in the application.

When should I apply for an IPO?

It is recommended to apply for an IPO after thorough research. Our tools can help you assess the right time based on market trends and IPO performance.
